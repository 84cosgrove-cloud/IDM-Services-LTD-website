About IDM

Specialist Telecoms & Network Engineering

Hands-on engineering expertise, digital project management, and a commitment to delivery in every mission-critical environment.

IDM Services NW is a specialist telecoms and network services company headquartered in the Liverpool region, supporting clients across the UK with agile, highly skilled engineering teams.

We work across mission-critical environments where uptime, responsiveness, and technical precision are essential. Our experience spans telecoms infrastructure, data centres, rail networks, industrial facilities, commercial spaces, and complex infrastructure developments.

Our operational model combines hands-on engineering expertise with digital project management systems to ensure efficient communication, real-time coordination, and reliable project completion. We are currently working towards ISO 9001 accreditation as part of our commitment to continuous improvement.
