Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover?
We're headquartered in the Liverpool region of North West England and deliver nationwide across the UK. Our engineering teams mobilise to any location for planned works, maintenance programmes and urgent callouts.
Do you carry out fibre splicing and testing?
Yes — fibre optic splicing, termination and OTDR testing are core services. We deliver fibre installation and testing across telecoms networks, data centres, FTTP build programmes and MDU (multi-dwelling unit) projects.
Can you handle MDU (multi-dwelling unit) installations?
Yes. We deliver internal fibre installation for apartment blocks and multi-tenant buildings — survey, install, splice, test and certification — with experience across UK FTTP build programmes.
Do you offer 24/7 emergency response?
Yes — we operate 24/7 rapid response and on-call engineering cover for fault finding, repair and urgent network restoration, including just-in-time response for uptime-sensitive data centre environments.
Can you work as a subcontractor on larger build programmes?
Yes. We regularly deliver as a specialist subcontractor on telecoms and infrastructure build programmes, with digitally managed workflows and full reporting visibility from mobilisation to completion. Get in touch to discuss capacity and coverage.
